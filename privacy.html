Privacy Policy

This privacy policy sets out how StudioGraphic ("we", "us", "our") uses and protects any information you give us when you use this website. We are committed to ensuring your privacy is protected.

What we collect

We may collect the following information when you submit an enquiry:

  • Your name and contact details (phone number and email address)
  • Details about your project or enquiry

How we use your information

We use your information solely to respond to your enquiry and provide the services you've requested. We will never sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties.

Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience and to understand how visitors use our site via Google Analytics. You can choose to accept or decline cookies using the banner shown when you first visit the site. Declining cookies will not prevent you from using the website.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyse how visitors use our website. This tool uses cookies to collect standard internet log information and visitor behaviour information in an anonymous form. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Your rights

You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, or to request its deletion at any time. To do so, contact us at info@studiographic.co.uk.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@studiographic.co.uk or call 07423 578 862.
